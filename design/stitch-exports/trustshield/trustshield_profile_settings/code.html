A Note on Data Sovereignty

In the architecture of TrustShield, we operate under the "Principle of Minimal Exposure." Your identity is not merely a record, but a cryptographic asset. We maintain that the individual remains the sole arbiter of their digital footprint. Our protocols are designed to ensure that while metadata facilitates function, your core identity remains shrouded behind sovereign-grade encryption.
